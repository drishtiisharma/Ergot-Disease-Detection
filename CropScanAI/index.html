Why Early Ergot Disease Detection Matters

Ergot fungus in millet can reduce crop yield by up to 50% and pose serious health risks if consumed. Our AI-powered tool enables early detection, helping farmers protect their fields, increase productivity, and ensure food safety. With climate change increasing disease prevalence, timely identification has never been more crucial.
